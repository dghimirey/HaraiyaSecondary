About Haraiya Secondary School

Established in 2024 BS, Haraiya Secondary School has been performing excellence in education and extra activities in Kanchan, Rupandehi. We are committed to providing quality education that nurtures intellectual growth, creativity, and character development.

Our school combines traditional values with modern teaching methodologies to prepare students for the challenges of tomorrow. We believe in holistic development, ensuring every student excels academically, socially, and personally.
